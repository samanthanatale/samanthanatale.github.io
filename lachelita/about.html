About La Chelita

La Chelita is a modern evolution of a beloved family restaurant, originally founded as La Tapatia by Graciela "Chelita" Ramirez in the 1970s. Led by her granddaughter Jessica, the restaurant blends traditional Jalisco recipes with fresh, Tex-Mex-inspired dishes that reflect her Mexican-American roots. As the neighborhood changes, La Chelita aims to stay true to its heritage while welcoming a new generation of diners; which serves as both a cultural touchstone and a vibrant community gathering space.

Meet the Owner

Jessica Ramirez is a third-generation chef honoring her grandmother's legacy while forging her own path. Born and raised in Texas, she blends tradition with innovation, respecting her cultural identity. Jessica is committed to keeping La Chelita accessible to longtime community members while adapting to the demands of a changing neighborhood, balancing authenticity, affordability, and growth with heart and purpose.
